# Stokos — Stripe Bank Onboarding (Client)

**Time required:** ~10 minutes  
**Who:** Stokos owner  
**When:** Anytime after Abassi creates Express account (can run parallel to TEST QA)

---

## Steps

1. Abassi emails you a **Stripe Express onboarding link**
2. Open the link in a browser
3. Confirm business name and details
4. Add **bank account** for payouts
5. Complete identity verification if Stripe prompts
6. Tell Abassi when finished

---

## You do NOT need

- Vercel, Clerk, or MongoDB access
- Stripe Dashboard (unless you want payout visibility later)

---

## After completion

Abassi confirms your account is ready, then switches the site to **live** payments. Azan will schedule a short staff training call.

See also [client-onboarding.md](./client-onboarding.md).
